Welcome To Eyo Festival

The Eyo Festival is one of the most iconic cultural celebrations in Lagos, Nigeria. Often called the “Adamu Orisha Play,” it reflects the deep spiritual and historical traditions of the Yoruba people. It is known for its majestic procession of white-robed masquerades that move through the streets of Lagos in honour of tradition and ancestry.

The white attire represents purity and peace, while the staffs carried by the Eyo symbolize authority, respect, and cultural continuity. The rhythmic drums, chants, and songs create a powerful atmosphere that transforms the city into a living cultural stage.

This website was created to educate, preserve, and celebrate the Eyo Festival while showcasing Lagos as a major cultural tourism destination in Africa.
