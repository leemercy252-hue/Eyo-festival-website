Festival Tickets & Visitor Guide

Everything you need to attend the Eyo Festival in Lagos

Experience The Eyo Festival Live

Every year, thousands of visitors gather in Lagos to witness the extraordinary Eyo Festival procession. From traditional drumming and dancing to the majestic white-robed masquerades, the festival offers a once-in-a-lifetime cultural experience.

Visitors from different parts of Nigeria and around the world travel to Lagos to experience the rich traditions of the Yoruba people. The streets become alive with music, celebration, and spiritual symbolism, making the festival one of the most important cultural attractions in West Africa.

How To Join The Festival

  • Register online before the festival date.
  • Choose a suitable ticket package.
  • Arrive early due to large crowds.
  • Follow all festival rules and instructions.
  • Respect local traditions and cultural practices.

Festival Rules & Guidelines

To preserve the cultural importance of the Eyo Festival, visitors are expected to follow important rules and traditions during the celebration.

  • No smoking during the main procession.
  • Respect all masquerades and festival performers.
  • Do not block the procession route.
  • Photography is allowed in designated areas only.
  • Visitors should dress modestly and respectfully.
  • Children should remain with guardians at all times.
  • Follow safety instructions from event officials.

Travel & Accommodation

Lagos offers a wide range of hotels, restaurants, transportation services, and tourist attractions for visitors attending the festival.

Visitors are encouraged to book accommodation early, as hotels become fully booked during major festival periods.
